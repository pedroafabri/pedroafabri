## About me ⚖️💾

Formerly a professional full-stack dev, I am currently transitioning my career into the **Legal field** (Law). 

Today, programming has returned to what it does best: being a fascinating hobby. I am a deep enthusiast of **retrodev, emulators, and retrohardware**, spending my free time exploring old hardware constraints and low-level code. 

Alongside my retro explorations, I also build **conventional gamedev projects**, developing modern indie games and mechanics just for the fun of crafting interactive experiences.

Most of my experimental, nostalgic, and over-engineered projects live under my fictional 90s tech company, **[Fabricom Systems](https://github.com/fabricom-systems)**.
